# 🧬 Welcome to the LTG GitHub Organization

The **Laboratory of Translational Genomics (LTG)** is part of the **Division of Cancer Epidemiology and Genetics (DCEG)** at the **National Cancer Institute (NCI)**.

LTG conducts research to understand how genetic variation influences cancer risk, progression, and outcomes. Our work integrates high-throughput genomic technologies with computational and statistical approaches to support translational cancer research.

🌐 **LTG Main Page:**  
https://dceg.cancer.gov/about/organization/tdrp/ltg
